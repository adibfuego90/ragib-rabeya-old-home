Morleen' miracle baby

A woman with dementia who has forgotten how to speak suddenly rediscovers her voice when paid a visit by a six-month-old baby.
Morleen Templeman, 83, has had trouble communicating since being diagnosed with dementia.
But when she saw 1 year old Lola, Mrs Templeman suddenly rediscovered her voice.
It blew everyone's mind away, … her transformation was incredible
And it is not just Mrs Templeman's speech that returns.She remembers how to take care for a child.
That long-term memory that's still there and that amazing maternal love she obviously had for her own children, then her grandchildren.
"It's that strength of maternal love and that deep long-term memory that comes to the fore.
"She was clearly an incredible mum."
