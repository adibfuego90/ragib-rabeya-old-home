Monica and her sweet Melodies

Everyone loves Monica. She has been with Nestem from past 5 years.
She is one successful singer of our small town and she is so kind that every weekend she comes and play for
our little children and Elders and she has been doing that from past five years.
She says that she loves to play for the people and especially for the exuberant audience.
Music is medicine for a soul, it fills everyone with hope and lively energy and thats how is Monica.
She has been supporting Nestem in every possible way and we all thank her for making our days lively with her music and melodious voice.
