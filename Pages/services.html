We Are Here At Your Service

We are providing all basic facilities. We work hard to provide a loving home and family for every child as well as older people in its care working hard to promote family relationships and impart a spirit of responsibility in every member of the organization.

Home

We are providing a safe home where they can live together with happiness. We have variety of rooms available according to their choice. Mainly we have two types of rooms, sharing room and private room for old people. For children, we have a common hall with number of beds.

Environment

We have well-maintained garden with plenty of plants and trees. For old people, we have provided benches and walking area. We are also providing playing area for children.

Nutrition


We are providing nutritious meal which includes fruit juice, meals and in between snacks with seasonal fruits and milk. We are totally take care of hygienity in the kitchen.

For the elderly special nutritional needs, we prepare selections that meet every nutritional chart requirement. Gluten-free meals and to manage sugar or salt intake are seprately prepared.

Home

Education is at the heart of our orphan care. It is the key to unlocking lasting and powerful change not only for a child, but the entire community. Every single one of our children attends school.

Environment

We value nursing care that respects human dignity. With the theme that love in nursing care has no boundaries, we operate nursing care facilities and provide nursing care services to our senior members.
