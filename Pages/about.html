About Us

Ragib Rabeya Old Home is a historic residence located in Goabari, House No: 32/A, Sylhet, Bangladesh. This old home holds significant cultural and architectural value, representing the heritage of the region. Built several decades ago, Ragib Rabeya Old Home has witnessed the passage of time and carries with it a rich history. Originally belonging to the Rabeya family, it has been passed down through generations, preserving the memories of the past. The house showcases traditional Bangladeshi architecture, featuring intricate woodwork, graceful arches, and beautifully carved details. The design reflects the craftsmanship and skills of artisans from the bygone era, which adds to its charm. Throughout its existence, Ragib Rabeya Old Home has witnessed various events, celebrations, and gatherings of family and friends. It has served as a place where people have come together, fostering bonds and strengthening relationships. Over the years, the house has seen changes and renovations to preserve its structural integrity while ensuring that its original essence remains intact. The old home stands as a testament to the enduring legacy of the Rabeya family and its contribution to the local heritage. Though no longer serving as a primary residence, Ragib Rabeya Old Home has become a point of interest for history enthusiasts and those interested in the architectural and cultural heritage of Bangladesh. It is a place that invites visitors to immerse themselves in the stories of the past and appreciate the beauty of its design. Today, Ragib Rabeya Old Home stands as a reminder of the rich history and cultural heritage of Sylhet, Bangladesh, continuing to inspire admiration and respect for its preservation and significance in the community.
